SOUTH FLORIDA TRAVEL GUIDE

WELCOME TO SOUTH FLORIDA

To find where to travel to, where to eat and places to visit, please look through our website for more information!

Always with great weather during any season of the year Florida is the best place to take a trip to. Specifically, South Florida remains one of the top tourist attractions for anyone coming to visit our Sunshine State! Once you book your plane tickets there are many other details of your trip to figure out and this website has it all. Let's start with the basics.
There are 3 different counties apart of South Florida and this website serves as a guide to all three! Within all of Palm Beach County, Broward County and Miami Dade County there are so many activities and places to each which you can find guides to on the other pages of this site.

How are you getting here?

There are 3 main airports to fly into all within an hour long drive of each other.
Palm Beach International Airport (PBI)
Fort Lauderdale-Hollywood International Airport (FLL)
Miami International Airport (MIA)
